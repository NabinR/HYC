Football

Football is the heart and soul of HYC since its establishment.

HYC recognises, values and nurtures football talents. We work together with our members and supporters to identify and develop football skills, physical and mental fitness, strength and conditioning and competitive amateur football.

HYC has been participating in community football tournaments locally and interstate since the beginning. All the players and coaches involved in the club are volunteers. HYC –Granville have many fantastic achievements in community football tournament history. With the support of our players, members and supporters we have managed to win multiple championships. We will continue to work hard to develop football in our youth community. We encourage our members and supporters to participate not only in football but in sporting activities and events which not only helps develop their physical strength but also improves mental health and wellbeing. We welcome all sports lovers to join us and build a strong youth community.
